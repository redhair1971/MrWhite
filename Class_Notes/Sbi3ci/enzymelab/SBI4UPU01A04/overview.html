Overview

So far you have studied some of the structures of biological molecules. You have studied the cell and how these molecules are involved in cell structure. You will now look at a very important class of molecules called enzymes. You will study how they work as biological catalysts. You will also have the opportunity to see how enzymes are important in foods.
