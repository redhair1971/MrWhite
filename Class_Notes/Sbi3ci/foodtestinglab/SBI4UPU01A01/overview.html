Overview

Welcome to your first activity in Grade 12 University level biology.  In this activity you will refresh your understanding of chemistry and study simple organic molecules.  You will build upon your understanding of chemistry and study large complex molecules that make up living things such as proteins, carbohydrates, lipids and nucleic acids.  Finally, you will apply your knowledge by putting large molecules together in chemical reactions and complete a food testing lab for the presence of macromolecules you have studied so far.
